Vehicle Customization

Ferrari offers an extensive range of customization options through their Tailor Made program. Owners can personalize their cars with unique paint colors, bespoke interiors, custom materials, and special finishes. This service allows customers to express their individuality.

Driving Experiences

Ferrari offers immersive driving experiences through events like Ferrari Driving Days and track experiences. These programs allow enthusiasts to drive various Ferrari models on racetracks under the guidance of professional instructors.

Maintenance and Repair

Ferrari provides specialized maintenance and repair services to ensure that every vehicle operates at peak performance. This includes regular servicing, software updates, and genuine Ferrari parts.

BRINGING FUTURE TO THE PRESENT

CONCEPT CARS

Ferrari has produced a handful of concept cars such as the Modulo, Mythos, and Pinin. Some of these were quite radical and never intended for production, while others showed styling elements that were later incorporated into production models. Most of Ferrari's concept cars have been collaborations with design studio Pininfarina. The most recent concept car to be produced by Ferrari themselves was the 2010 Millechili.

BIO FUEL and HYBRID

An F430 Spider that runs on ethanol was displayed at the 2008 Detroit Auto Show. At the 2010 Geneva Motor Show, Ferrari unveiled a hybrid version of their flagship 599. Called the "HY-KERS Concept", Ferrari's hybrid system adds more than 100 horsepower on top of the 599 Fiorano's 612 hp.[99] Also in mid-2014, the flagship LaFerrari was put into production featuring a hybrid system.
